javaBin is one of the largest communities in Norway. It is driven by volunteer enthusiasts from across the country. We have branches throughout the country – in Stavanger, Bergen, Trondheim, Vestfold, Southern Norway and Oslo. We organize events almost every month in all branches of javaBin. It is both meetups, events for students, events for kids, and other exciting stuff!

Our biggest happening of the year – JavaZone – takes place every September. That's right! One of the world's biggest Java conferences is run by a non-profit organization! 3,000 participants, 200 speakers, 50+ partners, and generally lots of knowledge sharing and fun!

We would like you to join one of our events! Also, if you are interested, you ask us about becoming one of the organizers. We have over 50 people who contribute to the community, creating events for our nearly 3,000 members! And we always looking for new friends!
