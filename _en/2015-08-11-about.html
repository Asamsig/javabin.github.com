javaBin is one of the largest community groups in Norway. It is driven by volunteer enthusiasts from across the country. We have branches throughout the country - Stavanger, Bergen, Trondheim, Vestfold, Southern Norway and Oslo. We organize events almost every month in all javaBin branches - both meetups, student events, events for kids, and much more exciting stuff!

That's right! One of the world's biggest Java conferences is run by an non-profit organization! 3,000 participants, 200 lecturers, 45 partner companies and general good mood!

Join a javaBin event! And if you are really interested, you can talk with us and become one of the organizers. We have over 50 people who contribute to create cool events for our nearly 3,000 members!
