javaBin is open for everyone who wants to become a member. Being a paying member means that you support the Java community in Norway and help us organize various events throughout the year, and JavaZone.

How much does a membership cost?

Membership in javaBin is included in the JavaZone ticket, so if you have bought a ticket to JavaZone or have received this from your employer, then you are already a member of javaBin for that year. If you are not going to JavaZone, you can buy a separate membership. Price for this is 500 NOK for a 12-month membership.

Students with a valid student ID can apply for free membership. If you are interested in a student membership, please send an email to medlemskap@java.no.

Validity

Membership is valid from the day it was purchased to the same date next year. This applies for both memberships accompanying JavaZone tickets, and for regular memberships.
